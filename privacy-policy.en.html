ShotCue Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-09 Last updated: 2026-07-09

ShotCue ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data we collect

Category Item How collected / where stored
Account (optional) Apple userIdentifier, name and email (only if you choose to share them at sign-in) Sign in with Apple. Stored only on your device (Keychain); never sent to our servers
Service usage Anonymous install identifier (random UUID), free-analysis usage count, first/last used timestamps Sent automatically with analysis requests; stored on our server
Payment App Store subscription receipt (JWS token) Included with Pro requests for verification only. Not stored on our servers (stateless verification)
Content The reference photo you submit (downsized JPEG with EXIF removed) plus capture values (focal length, gravity vector, face-tilt angle) Sent when you request analysis. Discarded immediately after analysis; never stored
Support Email address (optional, for replies), your message, app version, device model, OS version, hashed anonymous ID When you send a support email

We do NOT collect: location (GPS — location EXIF is stripped on-device before upload), contacts, full photo library index, IDFA (advertising ID), or national ID numbers. We use no crash-reporting or behavioral-analytics SDKs.

2. How we use your data

  • Provide camera-calibration guidance (analyze reference photos)
  • Manage the free-tier quota, verify subscription entitlement, and prevent abuse
  • Respond to your inquiries

3. Retention

Item Period
Apple userIdentifier, name, email Stored only on your device; removed immediately when you sign out or delete your account
Anonymous install identifier & free-usage records Kept to prevent abuse. Our server never stores the raw identifier — only a one-way pseudonymous key (HMAC). On account deletion (or email request) the account data (usage timestamps) is erased immediately; only the free-usage counter is retained under the pseudonymous key (separated storage) to prevent free-quota abuse, and it re-applies only if the same device uses the service again
Photo content Discarded from server memory immediately after analysis (never stored)
Analysis metadata Stored only on your device — not on our servers
Server access logs (request path, status code, latency — no personal identifiers) 90 days
Support emails 1 year after reply

4. Sharing with third parties

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing. Disclosure is made only when required by law.

5. Processors (data sub-processors)

Processor Purpose Region
Apple Inc. Sign in with Apple / App Store IAP US, EU
Analysis API (photo analysis, free-tier quota)

6. International data transfers

Sign-in and payment data are handled by Apple under Apple's transfer mechanisms. Declining these transfers limits sign-in and subscription features; core app features still work.

7. Children's privacy

The Service requires users to be at least 14 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 14. If we learn that we have, we will delete it without delay.
