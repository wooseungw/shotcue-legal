PhotoMimic Pro Subscription Terms

Effective date: 2026-07-09 Last updated: 2026-05-18

These terms comply with Apple App Store Review Guideline 3.1.2(a).

1. Subscription product

  • Name: PhotoMimic Pro
  • Product ID: com.photomimic.pro.monthly
  • Type: Auto-Renewable Subscription
  • Period: 1 month

2. Price

  • USD $3.99 / month (or local equivalent at App Store rates)
  • Apple may adjust pricing based on currency and tax.

3. Billing

  • Charged immediately on subscription start.
  • Charged automatically on renewal (unless canceled at least 24 hours before the next renewal date).

4. Auto-renewal

  • Subscriptions auto-renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the next renewal date.
  • Cancellation within 24 hours of renewal will not stop the current renewal charge.

5. How to cancel

You can cancel anytime: 1. Open Settings on your iPhone 2. Tap your Apple ID at the top 3. Tap Subscriptions 4. Select PhotoMimic Pro 5. Tap Cancel Subscription

After cancellation, Pro features remain available until the end of the current billing period.

6. Refunds

Refunds are handled by Apple, not by us. Submit refund requests via: - https://reportaproblem.apple.com - Or Apple Support

7. Free tier / On subscription expiry

  • Free tier: Up to 3 saved reference photos. Capture is unlimited.
  • On expiry: Reference photos beyond the latest 3 are automatically archived (not deleted). They are restored immediately when you resubscribe.

8. Family Sharing

Family Sharing is enabled for this subscription. One family member's subscription unlocks Pro for the family group.

9. Payment management

Payment is tied to your Apple ID. We never access your card details. Update payment info in Settings → Apple ID → Payment & Shipping.
